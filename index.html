Homeless Offerings by Organizations, or HOBO, is a need-based service locator within the DC area, aimed toward the homeless. First, HOBO displays your current location. Choosing one or more options of tired, hungry, sick, or in danger, HOBO then displays providers for said needs, and directions to such locations.

Financial support goes directly to shelters and other organizations in the DC area via PayPal.
